# Nykaa Sales & Customer Intelligence Dashboard

## Executive Summary
This project delivers a dual-view analytical dashboard designed to support business decision-making for Nykaa. The project focuses on two core pillars: **Sales Growth** and **Customer Retention**, providing actionable insights to optimize profitability and identify high-value demographics.

## Key Insights & Features
* **Sales Performance:** Tracks 27.59M in total revenue with a 38% profit margin, highlighting category performance across multiple sales channels.
* **Customer Retention:** Analyzes repeat vs. new customer behavior, helping to identify strategies for long-term loyalty.
* **Geographic & Demographic Segmentation:** Breaks down revenue by region and gender, enabling targeted marketing campaigns.
* **VIP Customer Identification:** Pinpoints high-value customers to facilitate personalized retention strategies.

## Tools & Technologies
* **Visualization:** Power BI
* **Data Preparation:** Power Query / Advanced Excel
* **Metrics Tracked:** Revenue, Profit Margin, Retention Rate, Average Revenue Per User (ARPU).

## Business Impact
The dashboard empowers stakeholders to shift from reactive reporting to proactive growth strategies by identifying top-performing categories and high-impact customer segments.
